Chief Clinical Officer

Dr. Amy Reale

SRP Approved Supervisor

Clinical & School Psychologist (USA/SG)

Dr. Amy is an American clinical and school psychologist trained in New York City, with over 2 decades of experience supporting children, adolescents, parents, and families across Singapore, Spain, and the United States.

She specializes in helping neurodivergent families navigate complex systems. She adopts a two-pronged approach: providing children with comprehensive neuropsychological assessments and executive functioning coaching, while equipping parents with practical tools to reduce family stress and sustain their own mental health.

Having conducted research in peri-natal mental health, Dr. Amy also has a specialized clinical focus in supporting parents navigating bonding difficulties, using observational methods and attachment research to strengthen the parent-child relationship.

"Dr. Amy's approach to diagnostic and psychological assessment is comprehensive. Her style is collaborative and neuro-affirming."
