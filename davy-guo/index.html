Dr. Davy Guo

Chartered Psychologist

Counseling Psychologist | DCounPsych, CPsychol

Dr. Davy holds a Doctorate in Counseling Psychology from the University of Manchester, UK. He is a Chartered Psychologist with the British Psychological Society, a licensed Psychotherapist certified by the Chinese Ministry of Health, and a board member of the Zhejiang Neuroscience Association Autism Professional Committee.

With over 15 years of experience across Canada, the UK, China, and Singapore, he specializes in supporting children and adolescents navigating anxiety, mood disorders, ADHD, and cross-cultural issues. His work includes therapeutic interventions, assessments, and social and academic support services, notably with special education centers like ELG.

Utilizing a range of therapeutic models—including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Person-Centered Therapy, Solution Focused Therapy, and Mindfulness—Dr. Davy provides clinical psychological and emotional support for international and bilingual schools.

In addition to his clinical practice, he has over a decade of experience providing targeted support for university students and is frequently invited by schools and organizations to conduct workshops and specialized training on a variety of mental health subjects.

"Dr. Davy's cross-cultural experience and adaptable therapeutic approach provide a supportive environment for children and adolescents navigating complex academic and emotional challenges."
