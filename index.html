The Nightingale Protocol

Coordinated Continuity of Care

Supported by data and clinical practice.

1

Understanding Your Needs

The journey begins with an initial 15-minute intake call. You do not need a diagnosis to start.

We use this time to understand your concerns and guide you to appropriate next steps, providing clarity on your clinical pathway.

Book Intake
2

Validating With Data

For many concerns, we start with a standardized screening process. Based on the screening results, the next steps may include an in-depth consultation, a diagnostic test, or classroom observations.

We support parents and children throughout the assessment pathway to ensure they are well-informed during the testing process.

3

Designing The Care Plan

Based on the data, we build an individualised care plan involving our multi-disciplinary team.

Our consultants include:

  • Psychologist
  • Art Therapist
  • Occupational Therapist
  • Couple Therapist
  • Executive Coach
  • Psychiatrist (Coming Soon)
4

Ongoing Education Through Masterclasses

Care continues after the sessions end. We provide support options to reinforce the strategies discussed.

This includes psychoeducation subscriptions with access to our audio masterclasses for accessible advice and parenting masterclasses.

To support your ongoing progress, Bonfire AI — your AI coach for self-regulation — can also check in with you automatically to help you build stronger self-regulation habits.

FAQ

Do I need a diagnosis before booking?

No. The intake is for families who are seeking a clinical pathway for their concerns.

What ages do you see?

We support children from babies or birth onwards, depending on the concern and pathway.

Do you assess other conditions besides ADHD?

Yes, we can assess a range of behavioral, emotional, learning, and developmental conditions. These include but are not limited to Autism, Learning Disabilities, Anxiety, Depression, and more.

Will you work with my child's school?

Yes, with your consent. We align next steps so support is consistent across settings.
