Psychological & Diagnostic Assessments

We use standardized, multi-modal psychometric tests to formulate clinical diagnoses. Pricing includes the testing sessions, consultations with other allied health providers, review of records, questionnaires, direct testing sessions, full psychological report, feedback session, and school observation if deemed necessary. The initial consultation is charged separately.

1. Cognitive Functioning

Measures verbal capabilities, reasoning, spatial organisation, short term memory and processing speed, all of which create a full-scale intelligence quotient score (IQ). Used for understanding learning potential, cognitive strengths and weaknesses, and school or programme applications.

Wechsler Scales WPPSI/WISC/WAIS >2 Hours Testing Time
Learn More

2. Academic Functioning

Compares cognitive functioning (IQ) with academic performance to identify strengths and weaknesses.

Wechsler Scales WIAT-4 >3 Hours Testing Time
Learn More

3. ADHD & Executive Function (Abbreviated)

A targeted assessment to identify most cases of ADHD. Clinical interview, records review, multi-informant ADHD diagnostic rating scales, social-emotional and adaptive questionnaires, and direct assessment of attention and executive functioning accounting for distracting environments. Note: If cognitive/academic testing is required, this moves to the Comprehensive category.

Conners 4 BASC-3 Vineland-3 BRIEF-2 >2 Hours Testing Time
Learn More

4. Comprehensive Neurodevelopmental Diagnostic

Full evaluation for complex presentations or multiple DSM-5 Neurodevelopmental Disorders, including: Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), Specific Learning Disorder (Dyslexia, Dyscalculia, Dysgraphia), Developmental Coordination Disorder (Dyspraxia), and Language Disorders.

Full Multi-Modal Battery >4 Hours Testing Time

The Diagnostic Process

A clinical pathway structured for review by schools and medical boards.

1

Initial Consultation (Required)

Every case begins with a 1-hour session with a Senior Consultant (S$330) to review developmental history and confirm the correct testing roadmap for your child.

2

Clinical Testing & Data

Standardized testing is conducted via secure video or in-clinic. We also collect multi-source data from parents and teachers using digital questionnaires.

3

Report & Feedback

You receive a full clinical report detailing findings and a "School Action Plan." A feedback session is included in the assessment fee to explain the results.

Important Clinical Protocol: Written consent is sought prior to the commencement of any diagnostic assessment to gather and share information from other sources. If consent is not granted, this might impact our capacity to provide a formal diagnosis, although confidentiality will be strictly maintained. The final clinical report is the property of the parent/guardian and will not be shared with schools or third parties unless explicitly requested by you.

Common Questions

Why is the Initial Consultation charged separately?

The consultation is a standalone clinical service to review history and determine medical necessity. Not every child requires a full battery, and this session ensures your child only undergoes the necessary testing.

Do I need a "Screener" or a "Diagnosis"?

A Screener indicates risk. A Diagnostic Assessment provides a formal clinical diagnosis. If you need exam accommodations (like extra time) or school placement support, schools typically require a full diagnostic report.

Is this accepted by International Schools?

Yes. We have experience offering services to a variety of international schools with varying curriculums and pedagogies (American, British, International Baccalaureate, etc.), and work closely with their learning support and pastoral care teams to provide relevant and appropriate recommendations.

Can this be done online?

For children aged 7+, most components (ADHD testing, neurodiversity testing, clinical interviews) can be conducted online. For Autism, we will provide a clear testing roadmap during the initial consultation session. All remote assessments use appropriate tools, trained facilitators, and relevant and secure software that maintain strict professional standards.
