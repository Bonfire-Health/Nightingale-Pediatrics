Financial FAQ

Can I use the Baby Bonus / CDA?

Our clinic operates as a private practice and is not currently listed as an Approved Institution (AI) for the Baby Bonus/CDA scheme. This allows us to maintain an independent clinical focus.

Does insurance cover these fees?

This depends entirely on your specific insurance policy. Some "Global Medical" or expat insurance plans do cover psychological assessments and therapy. We provide the necessary clinical invoices for you to submit a claim. We also provide referral letters for insurers, while detailed medical reports are available at an additional fee.

Do I need a referral letter?

No. As a private clinic, you can book directly with us. A referral letter is not required.
